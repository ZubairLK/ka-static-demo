Video Title : Fundamentals: Normative and Descriptive Claims

Video Description : We humans are a chatty bunch--we talk A LOT. But each claim we make can be sorted neatly into one of two categories: it either describes something or it evaluates something (philosophers call these descriptive and normative claims respectively). In this video Kelley illustrates the descriptive/normative distinction, and its importance, with the use of ample examples Speaker: Kelley Schiffman, Yale University
