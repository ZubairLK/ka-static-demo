Standard_identities

An identity is an equality, which is true for all values of the variables in the equality. Here we will learn three standard identities- (a+b)^2=a^2+b^2+2ab, (a-b)^2=a^2+b^2-2ab, (a+b)(a-b)=a^2-b^2.
